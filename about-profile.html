Company Profile

About B.E. Billimoria & Co. Ltd.

B.E. Billimoria & Co. Ltd. is a premier construction company established in 1974, specializing in ultra-premium construction projects across India. With over 50 years of excellence, we have built a reputation for delivering exceptional quality, innovative engineering solutions, and unmatched client satisfaction.

Our company operates with the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and technical expertise. We have successfully completed over 500 major projects, ranging from infrastructure development to commercial and residential complexes, earning the trust of government agencies, private corporations, and individual clients.

Our Heritage

Founded by Mr. B.E. Billimoria, our company began as a small construction firm with a vision to transform India's infrastructure landscape. Over the decades, we have grown into one of the most respected names in the construction industry, maintaining the founder's commitment to quality and innovation.

From our humble beginnings in Mumbai, we have expanded our operations across multiple states, building iconic structures that stand as testaments to our engineering excellence and architectural vision.
